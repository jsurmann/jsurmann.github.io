Brewing Beer

Why brew your own beer?

You don’t save money, for the price of the ingredients you can buy a finished beer in the supermarket. On top of that, it takes about 8 hours of work for 20 liters.

It tastes very good, and it makes a great gift. But in the end, there is one reason above all to brew your own beer: Cause it works.
