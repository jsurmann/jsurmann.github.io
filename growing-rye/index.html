Growing Rye

Since we have our own flour mill, I have been fascinated by grain. Small kernels that can be stored for a long time and become seed again when needed. In 2021 I decided to grow summer rye in our garden on 10 sqm. Over 3 kg of whole grain flour was the result.

Based on the good experience with the rye, I decided to plant sweet corn in 2022. The straw from the rye is reused as mulch.
